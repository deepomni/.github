### > SECURITY POLICY

HEY ;)<br>
security matters to us, and we really appreciate anyone who takes the time to responsibly report issues.

#### >> REPORTING SECURITY ISSUES

> please don't open public issues, pull requests, or discussions for security bugs,<br>
> that can put users at risk before we fix things.

<strong>JUST SHOOT AN EMAIL TO <a href="mailto:dev@deepomni.org">dev@deepomni.org</a>.</strong>

we'll get back to you within a couple of days (usually faster if it's urgent).

#### >> WHAT HELPS US MOVE FAST

- which version(s) you are seeing this issue.
- quick description of what's wrong.
- steps to reproduce (or small POC if you have one).
- impact of the issue in the perspective of attacker.
- preferred name/handle if you'd like credit later.

> <strong>WE PROMISE</strong>: no legal action against good-faith reporters who follow this process.

#### >> WHAT HAPPENS NEXT

- we acknowledge your report quickly (aiming for <72h).
- we'll keep you in the loop as we triage and fix.
- we coordinate disclosure with you no surprises.
